Star Organic Farm


Star Organic Farm is a well established company since 1988, It has the only green leaves farm certified as 100% organic. Star Organic Farm has taken to approach to do organic farming to supply high quality organic products to the consumers in order to bring their health standards to a maximum and to be an ethical organization in the farming and food sector. They are the inventors of cut organic leaves products and they used post harvesting techniques to produced fresh leaves and vegetables which can be easily prepared for the meals.




Food Safety Policy


We at STAR ORGANIC FARM. make every effort to ensure that the “Fresh Wholesome Minimal Process Green Leaves & Vegetables Products” being supplied to our buyers are free of all potential biological, chemical and physical hazards, by processing in an environment where ISO 22000:2005/HACCP principles have been established while complying to all applicable legal requirements. We believe that the continual improvement of our consumer safety approach is based on the commitment and contribution of our employees towards food safety and on upgrading their knowledge and skill on food safety.




Our Mission


Enrich the lives by providing sustainable products while reducing the impact on environment to ensure a healthy future of the people and the planet. Offer the goods with better choices to improve the quality of the lives in modern society.
