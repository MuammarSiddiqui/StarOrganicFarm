Description About this product:- Pomegranate is one of the most ancient cultivated trees traditionally used in folk medicine in the Middle East. Scientific investigations have provided evidence that the health properties of pomegranate mainly depend on the phytochemicals present in both the edible and non-edible portions of the fruit [1]. The juice contains a valuable quantity of anthocyanins, flavonoids, and hydroxyl benzoic acids [2] while the peel is rich in hydrolysable tannins, among which punicalagin is the dominant and peculiar compound [3]. Also, pomegranate seeds are a source of polyunsaturated fatty acids (PUFA), among which punicic acid (PA; cis-9 trans-11 cis-13 18:3) has been proven to be beneficial for human health
